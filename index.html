About Me

Hey y'all! I'm Katherine.

I'm an Audio Engineer turned Web Developer based in Atlanta.

I’m a Texas native, originally from San Antonio, with a deep love for the Spurs, Whataburger, and authentic Tex-Mex. After graduating with a Sound Design degree from SCAD in 2015, I moved to Atlanta to pursue a career in audio engineering for film and television. While working as a freelance editor, I began exploring my long-standing interest in coding. When I learned all I could on my own, I decided a classroom was the place I wanted to be. I’m currently a student in Georgia Tech’s Coding Bootcamp, staying serious about programming and working hard to learn as much as I can to become a developer.
